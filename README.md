## Billmora

Billmora is a free and open-source billing management software that can be adapted for hosting billing management, offering a flexible solution for businesses that need to handle recurring payments, invoicing, and customer billing operations. While it is not specifically designed as a dedicated hosting billing platform like WHMCS, its customizable and open-source nature makes it a viable option for hosting providers looking to manage their billing processes efficiently.

> [!WARNING]
> Billmora is still under development.
